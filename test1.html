Welcome to RecycleRight Qatar

RecycleRight Qatar is an educational website created as part of my MYP Personal Project. The aim of this website is to educate students, families, and communities about correct recycling practices in Qatar.

Recycling plays an important role in protecting the environment, reducing pollution, saving energy, and conserving natural resources. In Qatar, improving recycling habits supports the goals of Qatar National Vision 2030.

Why Recycling Matters

  • Reduces waste sent to landfills
  • Saves energy and natural resources
  • Protects land and marine life
  • Supports a sustainable future

What Can Be Recycled?

Not all waste belongs in the same bin. Sorting waste correctly helps recycling centers process materials more efficiently.

Recyclable Materials

  • Plastic: water bottles, food containers (clean and dry)
  • Paper: newspapers, notebooks, cardboard boxes
  • Metal: aluminum cans, tin cans
  • Glass: bottles and jars

Important Rules

  • Rinse items before recycling
  • Remove food waste
  • Do not mix recyclables with general waste

Recycling Tips

Small daily actions can make a big difference. These simple tips can help improve recycling habits at home and school.

  • Carry a reusable water bottle
  • Use reusable bags instead of plastic ones
  • Separate waste into correct bins
  • Reduce paper use by studying digitally
  • Reuse jars, boxes, and containers

At School

  • Use both sides of paper
  • Turn off lights and electronics
  • Encourage friends to recycle

Recycling Pledge

Taking a pledge is a great way to commit to positive environmental change. By making this promise, you agree to recycle responsibly every day.

  • I will recycle whenever possible
  • I will reduce single-use plastic
  • I will encourage others to recycle
